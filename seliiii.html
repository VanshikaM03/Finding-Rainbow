Support LGBTQIA+ owned small businesses

1. Automic Gold Small Seamless Huggies


Al, Automic Gold’s founder and designer, said they started making their own jewelry because “as a non-binary person I couldn’t find fine jewelry that wasn’t too feminine or masculine that I could fit or afford.” Al said all off the brand’s jewelry is genderless and size-inclusive — it’s made in-house from locally sourced reclaimed materials. It sells bracelets, rings, necklaces and earrings, as well as charms and body jewelry.


Queer India Mart


Queer india mart was started to provide all LGBTQ specific merchandise. They are associated with many artisans and craftsmen across India. Also, they work closely with Queer organizations and NGO’s. In the past, they have organized and collaborated with a few events

BeUnic India


BeUnic India, co-founded by Ashish Chopra, first runners-up Mr. Gay India (2018), is an e commerce marketplace for LGBTQ entrepreneurs and designers. This platform is a true example of ‘for the community, by the community, of the community’. All the beautiful products on the website are created by the queer community. BeUnic is expanding by the day and also caters to an international audience now!

Yogam Pottery


Balakrishna Vellepu, the founder of Yogam Pottery,is a part of the lgbtqia+ community.Yogam Pottery offers various short workshops for you to learn at home as well as at birthdays and corporate events. They send the pottery wheel and other equipment that you might need to your doorstep to offer you an immersive and authentic experience.Yogam Pottery began as a desire to create and celebrate passion and individuality.
